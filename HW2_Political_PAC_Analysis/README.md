# HW2 — Foreign Political PAC Contribution Analysis
**Course: INFO 526 | University of Arizona**

## 📌 Overview
Visualization analysis of foreign-connected Political Action Committee 
(PAC) contributions to US political parties from 1999 to 2022 — 
exploring trends, party distribution, and country-of-origin patterns.

## 📊 Dataset
Foreign Connected PAC contribution records (1999–2022) from OpenSecrets, 
covering amount contributed, party affiliation, country of origin, and 
election cycle.

## 🔑 Key Visualizations
- Time series of PAC contributions by party across election cycles
- Grouped bar charts comparing Democrat vs Republican contributions
- Country-of-origin contribution breakdowns
- Multi-panel comparative plots across years

## 🛠️ Tools
`R` `ggplot2` `dplyr` `tidyverse` `R Markdown`
