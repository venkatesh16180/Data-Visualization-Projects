# HW3 — COVID Survey & Income Distribution Visualization
**Course: INFO 526 | University of Arizona**

## 📌 Overview
Visualization of COVID-19 survey responses and income distribution 
data — exploring distributions, comparisons across groups, and 
patterns in survey response data using advanced ggplot2 techniques.

## 📊 Datasets
- `covid-survey.csv` — COVID-19 attitude and behavior survey responses
- `income.csv` — Income distribution data for demographic comparison

## 🔑 Key Visualizations
- Ridgeplots for distribution comparisons across groups
- Diverging bar charts for survey response analysis
- Side-by-side comparison plots
- Faceted multi-panel visualizations

## 🛠️ Tools
`R` `ggplot2` `ggridges` `dplyr` `tidyverse` `R Markdown`
